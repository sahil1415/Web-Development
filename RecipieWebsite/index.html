Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup Milk Powder
  2. 1/4 cup All-purpose Flour (Maida)
  3. 1/8 tsp Baking Soda
  4. 2 tbsp Ghee
  5. 2 tbsp Milk (adjust as needed)
  6. 1 cup Sugar
  7. 1 1/2 cups Water
  8. 4-5 Cardamom Pods (crushed)
  9. Oil or Ghee for frying

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Add ghee and milk to form a smooth dough. Rest for 10 minutes.
  3. In a pan, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and crushed cardamom pods. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Shape the dough into small balls without cracks.
  5. Deep fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown.
  6. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 2 hours before serving.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  1. 500g Chicken (cut into pieces)
  2. 2 cups Basmati Rice
  3. 3 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  4. 2 Onions (sliced)
  5. 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  6. 1 cup Yogurt
  7. 4 Green Chilies (slit)
  8. 2 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  9. Whole Spices: Bay Leaf, Cloves, Cinnamon, Cardamom
  10. 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  11. 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  12. 1 tsp Garam Masala
  13. Fresh Mint and Coriander Leaves
  14. Salt to taste

Recipe

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook rice until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add whole spices, and saut√© until aromatic.
  3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes are mushy.
  5. Mix in chicken, yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until chicken is tender.
  6. Layer the chicken curry and cooked rice in a pot. Add mint, coriander, and garam masala between layers.
  7. Seal the pot with a lid and cook on low flame for 20 minutes (Dum).
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
